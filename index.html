Medical Aesthetics

1.Botox: is a popular injectable treatment used to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles on the face. It works by temporarily paralyzing the muscles that cause these wrinkles, resulting in smoother, more youthful-looking skin.
2.Fillers: Dermal fillers are injectable treatments that add volume and lift to the face, lips, and other areas to restore a youthful appearance. VitalityMD Medical Spa offers a range of dermal fillers, including Revanesse.
3.Laser hair removal: Laser hair removal is a non-invasive method for removing unwanted hair from various body areas. It targets the hair follicles with a laser, damaging them and inhibiting future hair growth. Clinical lasers offered at VitalityMD can treat all skin types 1 – 6.
4.Microneedling with Radiofrequency: Microneedling is a minimally invasive treatment that uses a small device with tiny needles to create micro-channels on the skin to deliver custom cocktails & serums. Radiofrequency (RF) lifts & tightens skin, stimulates collagen and elastin production, leading to smoother, more even skin.
5.Laser Genesis & IPL: these non-ablative treatments are able to solve various skin issues with minimal to no downtime. Great for conditions such as acne rosacea, large pores, scars, pigment lesions, redness, acne, lines and more.
6.AviClear: is the first and original FDA and Health Canada-cleared energy device for the treatment of mild, moderate, and severe acne.
7.JetPeel: The JetPeel treatment is a non-invasive multi-step dermal infusion system that delivers immediate visible results without downtime or skin irritation. It includes lymph drainage, detoxification, exfoliation, and dermal infusions of bio-active serums and boosters that can penetrate the skin to 1.4mm WITHOUT NEEDLES.
8.Biologique Recherche Skin Treatments and Peels: This line is known worldwide as the magic potion of the skincare world. With results sworn to be sent from heaven, in reality, the brand owes its product’s success to years of scientific research and a personalized skin analysis for each client. Recherche’s personalized treatments – whether for the face, body or scalp are equally suitable for both men and women.
9.Exosomes: Exosomes are the latest and most advanced treatment that can harness the power of over 2 billion stem cells. Exosomes are the cell communicators; they tell cells what to do and transfer numerous growth factors, cytokines and genetic materials that have shown signs of tissue repair and improving skin cells.
10.PRP: Cosmetic PRP, also known as Platelet-Rich Plasma, repairs your skin at the cellular level using your very own plasma. It contains a very high volume of platelets, inflammatory mediators and growth factors.

How To Choose the Right Medical Spa

Choosing the right medical spa is essential to ensure you receive safe and effective treatments. Here are some key factors to consider:

Qualifications and certifications: It is essential to research the qualifications and credentials of the staff at the medical spa. Look for clinics that employ licensed medical professionals such as doctors, nurses, and aestheticians. Check for their training and experience in the specific treatments you are interested in.

Reputation and client reviews: Research the reputation of the medical spa by reading client reviews online or asking for referrals from friends and family. Look for clinics that have a history of positive client experiences and are known for delivering excellent results.

Range of services: Look for a medical spa that offers a wide range of services. This ensures you access the latest and most effective treatments in the industry.

Cleanliness and hygiene: Cleanliness and hygiene are crucial for any medical facility, including a medical spa. Ensure that the clinic adheres to strict cleanliness and sanitation standards and uses FDA/Health Canada approved state-of-the-art equipment to ensure the safety of its clients.

Consultation process: Choose a medical spa that provides an in-depth, personalized consultation for clients. The consultation should include a discussion of your needs, medical history, and recommended treatment options. This helps ensure that you receive customized and effective treatments tailored to your needs.
